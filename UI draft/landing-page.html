Frequently asked questions

Does BPOS work offline?

Yes. You can continue selling during outages, and data syncs automatically when the connection returns.

Which printers and scanners are supported?

Most popular USB/Bluetooth barcode scanners and 58mm/80mm thermal printers work out of the box.

Can I connect to my online store?

Yes. BPOS integrates with OpenCart and other platforms to sync products, customers, and orders.

How do I migrate from my current POS?

We provide import templates for products, stock, and customers, plus a guided onboarding call.
